Overview

Purpose

As a rental company, JeepsKauai believes that we can offer a more unique jeep rental experience than the competitor. We aim to customize the jeep rental business for those who want to experience Kauai. With our local knowledge of the island and ability to direct customers to great hikes, beaches, outings and views. We also want them to not waste any of their precious vacation time going around renting other equipment, or not knowing where to visit while here on island. We believe that those with greater knowledge and experience about the Island can share this knowledge with others to improve their capabilities to enjoy the island safely. Allowing a greater number of people to experience and enjoy the Island and the beauty it holds. Our website will provide the ability to rent, both jeeps and adventure equipment and provide a way to leave reviews from experiences with us.

Audience

Our ideal customer is a Kauai tourist who would like to arrive to Kauai and be ready for adventure the moment they step into the rented Jeep. This is ideal for two reasons: One it does not waste any of the renter’s time, searching around for equipment or a capable vehicle. Many tourists’ customers arrive on the island with an idea of the things they would like to do while here on island, such as but not limited to, hiking, snorkeling, Stand up paddle boarding, kayaking, beach days, boogie boarding, surfing, etc.

Content

Home page

The content on my home page will be showing off our jeeps and beautiful island. Information as to what we do as a rental company. Jeeps Kauai will provide knowledge about the island and help visitors feel at home. we will have options to rent other gear with their jeep such as stand-up paddle boards, boogie boards, snorkel equipment and surfboards. We also want to provide to information as to where the best hikes are and what beaches have the waves and where it is the safest. We will provide knowledge about the dos and don’ts of the island to ensure a pleasant stay. We will provide family friendly hikes such as the Sleeping Giant Trail. Or for the more adventurous the Kalalau Trail. Some examples would be don’t drive in between people sitting on the beach and the water no matter how far they are from the shoreline. The home page will have links to the other two pages. From the huge sands of Polihale to the cool river of Wailua we want Kauai Jeeps to be part of every adventure Kauai has to offer.

Rates

The content on the rates page of the website will be about prices and packages for our rental equipment and vehicles. Our Jeeps will start off at one hundred and twenty dollars a day. We will have add-ons. premium surfboards and paddle boards 1 day rental will be $40. For 2-day rental $75. For 3-day rental $110. For 4-day rental $140. For 5-7 day $160. For a 2-week rental $300. We will also have an option for basic surfboards. 1 day rental at $30. 2-day rental at $55. 3-day rental at $75. 4-day rental at $95. For 5 - 7-day rentals it will be $120. A 2-week rental for $220. We will have body boards and beach chairs for 1 day rental $7, 1 week rental $25, and 2-week rental $45. We will also have options to rent snorkel equipment with a $12 a day or $84 a week. It will come with fins, snorkel, and a silicone mask.
